PPT Presentation Competition

Visual impact meets professional eloquence.

Date 05 May 2026
Time 1:00 PM - 2:00 PM

PPT Guidelines

  • Good Attire: All participants must dress in appropriate professional business attire. A polished and professional appearance contributes to the overall presentation scoring.
  • Slide Count: To prevent "death by PowerPoint," you might limit slides to a maximum of 10-12.
  • Content & Ethics: (A new rule) Content must adhere to academic integrity. AI-generated text or graphics must be clearly cited as such.
  • The 6x6 Rule: Encourage clarity by suggesting no more than 6 lines of text per slide and 6 words per line.
  • Media Usage: Embedded videos are permitted but cannot exceed 30 seconds of the total presentation time.
  • Duration: Each presentation must be 5-7 minutes long, followed by a 2-minute Q&A session. A 1-minute grace period is allowed before point deductions apply.
  • File Format: All presentations must be submitted in PDF or PPTX format at least 24 hours before the event.
