Event Structure & Levels

Level 1: Word Building Challenge

Rules: Start with a word given by the organizers. Participants must write new words starting with the last letter of the previous word.

Time Limit: 5 Minutes

Criteria: Participants must write at least 5 valid words. Those with the highest number of valid words will qualify for the next level.

Level 2: Mystery Round

To be revealed after Level 1 completion.

Level 3: Grand Finale

The ultimate challenge for the top qualifiers.

General Rules

  1. Participation is in teams of maximum 2 members.
  2. Levels 2 and 3 will only be revealed to those who clear the previous level.
  3. Participants must complete the tasks within the allotted time.
  4. Judges’ decision will be final.
  5. Rule violations may lead to disqualification.
