GURU'S ATHENAEUM 25-26

We don't want a club where people just stand and talk. We want a club that creates experiences. No stage fear. No pressure. Just pure creativity.

Why Join Our Events?

Connect & Collaborate

Meet fellow creative minds, build meaningful connections, and collaborate on exciting literary projects in a supportive community.

Build Confidence

Overcome stage fear in a judgment-free zone. Showcase your talent, receive recognition, and grow as a creative individual.

Win Recognition

Compete for prizes, certificates, and recognition. Every participant is celebrated for their unique contribution.

General Guidelines

•

Participants must report to the venue (ICS Lab) at least 15 minutes before the scheduled time.

•

Students should carry their college ID cards at all times.

•

Maintain discipline, decorum, and punctuality throughout the event.

•

Participants must adhere to the instructions given by the coordinators and judges.

•

Use of mobile phones is strictly restricted during the event unless permitted.

•

Any form of plagiarism or unfair practice will lead to disqualification.

•

Judges' decisions will be final and binding.

•

Participants are expected to respect fellow participants and maintain a positive environment.
